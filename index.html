Print on Demand

Order a print. No class needed.

Upload your own file or pick any project from our library — we handle the printing. Collect from the lab when it's ready.

📋
Pick a project or upload your file

Browse our Standard or Premium project library and choose what you'd like to print. Or bring your own STL file — we'll quote you based on size and material.

💬
We confirm your order

Send us a request via email or WhatsApp. We'll confirm the print time, colour, and cost before we start — no surprises.

🖨️
We print it for you

Our printers get to work. All filament, build plate prep, and post-processing are included. Usually ready within 1–2 business days.

📦
Collect from the lab

Pick up your finished print at the lab at a time that suits you. You can also add a class to learn how it was made.
