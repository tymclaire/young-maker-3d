3D printing & filament

What is 3D printing?

Everything you need to know before your first session.

🖨️
FDM Printing
Most common

Fused filament — builds objects layer by layer from plastic filament. Great for functional parts, toys, keychains, and decorative objects.

🌈
Multi-colour
AMS system

Our Bambu Lab printers use AMS (Automatic Material System) to switch between up to 4 colours mid-print — producing vibrant, multi-tone creations automatically.

🧵
PLA Filament
Beginner friendly

PLA (Polylactic Acid) is our primary material — biodegradable, easy to print, available in 30+ colours including matte, silk, glow-in-the-dark, and metallic finishes.
